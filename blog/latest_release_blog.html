Apple’s Massive Product Launch Week – March 2026

Apple introduced several new devices this week including a new iPhone, MacBooks and iPads. Here’s everything announced during Apple's latest product releases.

iPhone 17e

Price in India: ₹64,900

Apple introduced the iPhone 17e as the most affordable iPhone in the lineup. It includes the powerful A19 chip, improved battery life, MagSafe support and a 48MP camera.

MacBook Neo

Price in India: ₹69,990

MacBook Neo is Apple’s new entry-level laptop designed for students and everyday users. It offers long battery life and smooth performance for work and study.

MacBook Air (M5)

Price in India: ₹1,19,900

The MacBook Air now comes with the powerful M5 chip delivering faster graphics, improved AI processing and longer battery life while keeping the ultra-thin design.

iPad Air (M4)

Price in India: ₹64,900

Apple upgraded the iPad Air with the M4 chip making it significantly faster. It supports Apple Pencil and Magic Keyboard for productivity and creativity.
